Ukraine war latest live: 7 dead, 8 injured as Russia strikes Donetsk Oblast city market

Cluster Munitions Used in Attack on Druzhkivka

Russian forces attacked the city of Druzhkivka in Donetsk Oblast on February 4, according to regional authorities.

The attack killed seven people and left eight others injured,as first responders continued rescue operations at the site.

According to Governor Vadym Filashkin, the city’s market — a place where residents gather to buy essential supplies — was directly hit.

"This market was one of the last places where people felt a sense of normal life," said local officials.

Druzhkivka lies between Kramatorsk and Kostiantynivka and remains one of the key population centers under Ukrainian control in Donetsk Oblast.
